# Northstar Analytics Security Questionnaire

## Company overview
Northstar Analytics provides AI-assisted revenue segmentation and account scoring.

## Data processed
Customer names, customer email addresses, CRM opportunity history, sales activity notes, and usage analytics tied to named users.

## Security controls
- Encryption in transit: Yes
- Encryption at rest: Yes
- SSO/SAML: Yes
- Role-based access controls: Yes
- Audit logs: Yes

## SOC 2
SOC 2 Type I available. SOC 2 Type II: Not provided.

## Incident response
Incident response policy summary available upon request.

## Data retention
Default retention is 36 months after contract end unless otherwise negotiated.

## Subprocessors
AWS (US), Segment (US), Clearbit (EU). Full subprocessor list provided in product portal, not attached.

## AI/model training
Northstar does not train foundation models on customer data. Customer data may be used for service improvement and account-level recommendation tuning unless disabled.

## Gaps
Data processing agreement: Not provided.
Penetration test summary: Not provided.
