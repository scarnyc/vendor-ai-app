# Finance Approval Matrix

## Spend approval thresholds

Approval is based on annual contract value unless total contract value or budget status creates a higher-risk condition.

| Annual Contract Value | Required approval |
|---:|---|
| $0 - $25,000 | Business owner |
| > $25,000 - $50,000 | Business owner and Procurement manager |
| > $50,000 - $100,000 | Business owner, Procurement manager, and VP Finance |
| > $100,000 - $250,000 | Business owner, Procurement manager, VP Finance, and CFO |
| > $250,000 | Business owner, Procurement manager, CFO, and Executive sponsor |

## Total contract value

Total contract value should be calculated as:

Annual contract value x contract term in months / 12

One-time implementation fees should be identified separately if present in the quote.

## Budget status

If the available budget for the cost center is lower than the annual contract value, Finance approval is required even when the annual contract value is below normal Finance approval thresholds.

If a vendor request has no cost center, no budget owner, or unclear budget status, the request should be routed to Finance or FP&A before approval.

## Contract term

Contracts longer than 24 months require Finance review because they create a multi-year spend commitment.

## Payment terms

Net 30 is standard.

Net 45 requires Procurement manager review.

Net 60 requires VP Finance review.

Payment terms longer than Net 60 require VP Finance and Legal review.
